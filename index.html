My favourite Hobby

Playing Football

Football is one of my favourite hobbies. I love playing it with my friends when I'm free. It's a sport played with a ball. You have to kick the ball, without touching it to your hands. You have to score in the opponents goal the score a point. There is a goalkeeper in each team who can use their hands in the D-Box to save the ball from going in the goal. It's a lot of fun if you have 5 or more players.
